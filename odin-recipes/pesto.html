Pesto

Description

Basil pesto is a traditional italian recipe, that requires little ingredients but makes sure to extract all the flavor and properties in them.

Ingredients

  • Extra virgin Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Basil
  • Pecorino Cheese
  • Pinions
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Add the Garlic to the mortar and grind it, you can help the process using salt.
  2. Once it's a paste, add the pinions and grind.
  3. When both ingredients are integrated, start adding basil until you have no more left.
  4. Add olive oil while you grind the basil so you can slow the oxidation of it, once you serve it add the pecorino cheese.
