Pizza Napolitana

Description

Traditional Neapolitan Pizza is not what we are used to eat when we go out, it's crispy but light and airy crusted, the most common is the Margherita.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • water
  • salt
  • Yeast
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • olive Oil
  • basil

Steps for the dough

  1. Mix flour, water, salt and yeast into a shaggy mass until it's uniform.
  2. Cover the bowl with cling film and leave it to rest for 12 hours
  3. Cut the dougs in equal parts, around 250g each.

Steps for the cooking

  1. Top the base of the streched dough with tomato sauce.
  2. Spread a handful of mozarella acros the pizza.
  3. You can add the basil and olive oil before or after cooking.
  4. Slide the pizza into a high heat oven and take it out when you notice it's crispy.
